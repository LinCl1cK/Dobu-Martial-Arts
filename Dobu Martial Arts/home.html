What is DOBU?

DOBU stands for (Dynamic Offensive Breakthrough Unleashed).

DOBU (Dynamic Offensive Breakthrough Unleashed) is a martial art that unleashes the beast within. It trains its practitioners to tap into their primal instincts and unleash their full potential in combat, using explosive movements and powerful strikes to overwhelm and dominate the opponent. DOBU also teaches its practitioners the values of determination, opportunity, balance, and unity, which guide them in their martial art and in their life. DOBU is more than a martial art, it is a way of life.
