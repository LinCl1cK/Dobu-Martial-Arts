Training Program

DOBU Martial Arts is a martial arts organization that aims to train its members in various disciplines of combat, such as Jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo, and Muay Thai, and more. The training program of DOBU Martial Arts consists of the following elements:

Classes: DoBu Martial Arts offers classes for different levels of skill and experience, from beginners to advanced. The classes are taught by qualified and experienced coaches, who provide instruction, feedback, and guidance to the students. The classes cover the techniques, principles, and strategies of each martial art, as well as the physical and mental aspects of training. The classes are held in a large, matted martial arts area, equipped with punching bags, pads, and other training equipment.

Fitness Training: DoBu Martial Arts also offers fitness training for its members, to help them improve their strength, endurance, agility, and flexibility. The fitness training is conducted by professional fitness coaches, who design customized programs for each individual based on their goals and needs. The fitness training is done in a fully-equipped gym, which has cardio machines, weights, resistance bands, and other fitness tools.

Self-Defense: DoBu Martial Arts offers a comprehensive self-defense course that incorporates techniques from various martial arts. The program is designed to equip students with practical skills for personal protection, covering basic movements, blocking, kicking, punching, basic falling, and striking techniques. With an emphasis on building confidence, awareness, fitness, and self-discipline, the course caters to individuals of all skill levels, making it an ideal choice for anyone looking to enhance their self-defense capabilities.
